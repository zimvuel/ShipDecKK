About Us

Our History

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK quickly established itself as a leading ship dealership specializing in high-end yachts, cargo ships, and luxury liners. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every vessel we build and sell.

We use only the finest materials and work with expert craftsmen to ensure that each ship meets the highest standards of quality and performance. Our reputation for reliability and customer satisfaction has made us a top choice in the maritime industry.

At ShipDecKK, we understand that buying a ship is a significant investment. That's why we provide unparalleled customer service throughout the entire process, from initial inquiry to delivery and beyond.
